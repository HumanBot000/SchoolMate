# SchoolMate 🎓

SchoolMate is a mobile school planner designed to help students organize their daily classes, assignments, and grades in a single, intuitive interface.

---

## Features

- **Personalized Dashboard**: Get an overview of your school day, including active lesson tracking and holiday countdowns.
- **Interactive Schedule**: Plan your week with alternating week support and conflict-free class scheduling.
- **Homework Organizer**: Track assignments with customizable reminder notifications and a structured task list.
- **Grades & Statistics**: Track your academic progress with automatic grade calculation, supporting standard numeric grades (1.0 - 6.0) and points-based scales (0 - 15).
- **Holidays**: View upcoming school holidays calculated dynamically based on your region.
